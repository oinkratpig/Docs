Flower Power

Rules

  • Fear & Hunger difficulty.
  • Any ending allowed.
  • NG+ skills are not allowed.
  • Playing intro is not allowed.
  • Advantageous mods or cheats are not allowed.
  • Party members ARE allowed, but they must follow the rules of the challenge. Auto-attacking party members such as Black Kalev, Ghouls, and Blood Golem are not allowed in combat. Black Kalev is allowed for sacrificing as long as he has not dealt any damage in combat.
  • Must play as Olivia.
  • The only damage allowed in the run must come from Hemlock or Condensed Hemlock.
  • Items/spells are allowed if they do not deal damage. Spells that theoretically can deal damage but deal 0 are not allowed.
  • Damage dealt outside of combat (such as from guns or bear traps) is not allowed.

+1 Gro-goroth and Sylvian if Radiating Soul skills have been unlocked.
