Background story

Trenchguns

Disarming opponents before battle isn't very useful if you still need to use ammo in the fight to kill them. Trenchguns are easier to kill with before combat starts. More ammo-efficient than the other options.

Go back to train and head north. Loot east Old Town. Talk moonscorched out of fighting.

Notes

Marcoh, Tanaka, Marina, and Olivia are vulnerable at the start of the game. Before attacking them, Abella must leave the train. Marcoh must be fought first, and then Tanaka. The other two contestants are then vulnerable.

Poison Tip only works on Bayonet. It DOES NOT work on other firearms.

Aiming for the head is viable with firearms - still chance to miss, but significantly higher than melee.

Mob wastes too much ammo to deal with. Use sewers to navigate.

3 Rev only in combat so ammo isn't wasted.

Lugr ammo can be purchased in Old Town.

Essential Skills

Prevents dying at >1 HP. Essential for consistency against certain bosses (like Platoon).

Hardened Heart

Consistent healing is essential. Both rings have pros and cons.

Leechmonger generally results in more healing and works well with Counter Stance (specifically when going against non-magic attacks). Has no effect if you aren't hitting the enemy (such as DOT cheese spamming block + heals).

Ring of Wraiths is consistent and will always give healing no matter what you're spending your turns doing, although its healing is pretty weak compared to Leechmonger.

If you have not found a Bayonet, Eyeglasses should result in more consistent headshots.

Rules

  • Fear & Hunger difficulty.
  • Must play Levi, any past is allowed.
  • Cannot deal damage to enemies with anything other than firearms. Bayonet is permitted.
  • DOT is allowed only if applied by a Bayonet.
  • The following are NOT allowed:
    • Bear traps.
    • Glass shards.
    • DOT-applying items (such as Ornamental Lantern, Hemlock, etc.).
    • Blood Golem and Necromancy.
    • Golden rule - any instance of damage coming from something other than a firearm is not allowed.
  • Cannot recruit any party members at any time, even for sacrifices.
  • No advantageous mods or cheats obviously.
  • Any ending allowed.
  • NG+ skills not allowed.

+1 Gro-goroth and Sylvian if Radiating Soul skills have been unlocked.
