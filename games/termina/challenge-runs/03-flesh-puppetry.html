Progression Plan

Starting Character

O'saa because you start with a Soul Stone, lots of items, more max HP, affinity, and a Skin Bible. Also means you don't have to kill him, which is nice due to him being one of the harder contestant fights.

Since guns are unusable, his downsides are irrelevant

Choose:

  • Soul Stone
  • Learn of worship in Europa -> Sylvian
  • Learn basic techniques / advanced magic (doesn't matter)
  • Skin Bible - Sylvian
  • Iron Spear (more Attack meaning more Flesh Puppetry damage)

Make sure that Needles doesn't kill any contestants before you can grab their soul.

Fighting enemies before getting a Betel's stone is not viable whatsoever. The chance they attack the limb you want is almost nothing - you will almost certainly run out of Mind before killing the enemy.

Ending A

  • PROS:
    • Easiest to get to. Not every contestant needs to be killed, and the only hurdle is the Moonless fight which isn't very difficult.
  • CONS:
    • Platoon will take 20 years to kill.
    • Logic has a bajillion health (even with DOT it takes a long time to kill her).
    • Logic counters Sisu / Hardened Heart due to Moth Swarm being a massive multi-attack.
    • Logic (first phase) and Kaiser both deal AOE which means party member can't tank everything.

Ending B

  • PROS:
    • Second easiest ending to get to. Lots of time to kill contestants.
  • CONS:
    • No party members to tank.
    • Rher sapping Mind is a huge issue. Fight might last too long for Mind restoration to keep up.
    • Per'kele counters Sisu / Hardened Heart for a good portion of the fight.

Ending C

  • PROS:
    • Per'kele is the only final boss and he's not super difficult.
    • Many GoF&H shrines will have to be created for the affinity, meaning this ending is easier to obtain.
  • CONS:
    • No party members to tank.
    • Per'kele counters Sisu / Hardened Heart for a good portion of the fight.

Rules

  • Fear & Hunger difficulty.
  • Any ending allowed.
  • NG+ skills are not allowed.
  • Playing intro is not allowed.
  • Advantageous mods or cheats are not allowed.
  • Party members ARE allowed, but they must follow the rules of the challenge. Auto-attacking party members such as Black Kalev, Ghouls, and Blood Golem are not allowed in combat. Black Kalev is allowed for sacrificing as long as he has not dealt any damage in combat.
  • The only forms of damage allowed are the following:
    • Flesh Puppetry
    • Obviously mind-controlled party members dealing damage is allowed.
  • If something does not deal damage, it is allowed in combat (Loving Whispers, Nettle, etc.).
  • If something can deal damage but still deals 0, such as Mischief of Rats, it is still not allowed.
  • DOT, guns, etc. are not allowed as they technically deal damage.
  • Bear Traps are not allowed on enemies that they deal damage to (for example, they are allowed to be used on Death Masks because they do not deal damage).
