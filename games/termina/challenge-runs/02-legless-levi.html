Kill Karin for soul. Persuasion + Displomacy are essential for dealing with Bobbies.

Save at train

Diplomacy + Persuasion

Makes many enemies a breeze and essential for fighting Bobbies with only one party member.

Pheromones

Start with Sylvian affinity to learn ASAP. Use on white-spiced Blood Golem.

Small Thing's Amulet

(Preferably 2 of them)

Can be bought from Dr. Kefer's shop and found sometimes after fighting the ratkin gank. Agility is essential to get ASAP.

Essentials

Rushing sewers -> orphanage is ideal so that the GoF&H skin bible can be found ASAP.

Get at least 10 agility ASAP.

Spice Forge

Obviously this is just an amazing ability in general, but it's primarily needed for Blood Golem to spawn every fight immediately.

Blood Golem

Tanks hits for you.

Warding Sigil

Because running away from enemies is near impossible sometimes, Warding Sigil is absolutely essential.

Engrave

Agility is the biggest weakness in this run (besides how mind-numbingly tedious the run is). Engrave with GoF&H.

Guns ARE NOT useable in the overworld. They are, however, usable in combat.

There are some enemies that move faster than you and are therefore unavoidable if chasing. Avoid encountering altogether unless they can be killed.

Rules

  • Fear & Hunger difficulty.
  • Any ending allowed.
  • NG+ skills are not allowed.
  • Playing intro is not allowed.
  • Advantageous mods or cheats are not allowed.
  • No party members allowed at any point, even for sacrifices. This excludes Blood Golem and Necromancy, which are allowed.
  • Must play Levi.
  • Backstory must not be skipped and "Take the mission and fulfil your duty" must be chosen. Other choices are up to the player.
  • At the start of the run, the player must immediately run to east Old Town and step on a bear trap. You are free to loot on the way to the bear trap.
  • Heroin is not allowed to be used at any point.
  • If a Sylvian circle is created, you must immediately amputate both legs with a bonesaw.
